New age of Ayurveda

India has used Ayurveda for thousands of years, but now, research
has introduced the whole world to ayurveda which is now
called modern ayurveda or simply just ayurveda. But there are differences between
ayurveda in the past and modern ayurveda. For Example, in the past, medicine was
given in the form of powder and nowadays, ayurvedic medicine could be given
as a syrup, tablet, or capsule.
