Benefits of Modern Ayurveda

Ayurveda is categorised into three different types of life forces called Doshas which is named Vata dosha, Pitta dosha and Kapha dosha. It's believed that every human has one of the doshas of life energy. An Ayurvedic doctor will first identify which dosha you belong to and then decides the treatment. For centuries Ayurveda has been finding solutions to many problems that affect us. Every solution gives the body a wide range of benefits.
