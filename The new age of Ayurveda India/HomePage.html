What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda, is a healthy healing system (traditional medicine) which consists of yoga, massage, acupuncture and herbal medicine that people in India have used for thousands of years.

"Ayurveda" is a Sanskrit word which comes from two Sanskrit words: Ayur and Veda. Ayur means life and Veda means Science or Knowledge
